# Chaparral Aerospace and Rocketry Association

## Welcome

Our goal at CARA is to translate the theoretical aspects of chemistry, mathematics, physics, and engineering into practical, real-world knowledge. We would like to explore the general field of aerospace and the specific field of rocketry. Our interests lie in retracing what has worked in the past and in developing a rocket engine based on our own data and calculations.

For more information please email [cara@cod.edu](mailto:cara@cod.edu).
